Research Projects

ECoDe: A Sample Efficient Method for Co-Design of Robotic Agents

Australasian Joint Conference on Aritificial Intelligence, 2024

Authors: Kishan R. Nagiredla, Buddhika L. Semage, Arun Kumar AV, Thommen G. Karimpanal, Santu Rana

ECoDe proposes a multi-fidelity-based exploration strategy in which we tie the controllers learned across the design spaces through a universal policy learner for warm-starting subsequent controller learning problems, to improve the sample efficiency in the co-design process.

View Publication

RoboNet: A Sample Efficient Robot Co-Design Generator

MAPoDeL Workshop of CoRL, 2024

Authors: Kishan R. Nagiredla, Arun Kumar AV, Thommen G. Karimpanal, Santu Rana

RoboNet is a combination of GFlowNet with rate-based design prioritization and cost-aware sampling strategy to evaluate robots based on future promise under full training, thus, using less samples to determine best co-designs for a given task.
